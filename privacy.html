Privacy Policy

HandsOnMoney is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how I handle your data when you use the app.

Data Collection: HandsOnMoney does not collect any personal or financial data from you. The app does not collect your name, email address, or any financial information you enter into the app.

Usage Statistics: HandsOnMoney collects anonymous usage statistics to help me improve the app. This data does not include any personal information and is used solely to enhance the user experience.

Data Transmission: HandsOnMoney does not send your financial data to any remote servers. All financial data remains stored locally on your device.

Internet Connection: HandsOnMoney does not require an internet connection to function. You can use the app entirely offline.

Changes to This Policy: I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact: If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact me at vitaliy dot pianykh at gmail.com.
