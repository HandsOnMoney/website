Dashboard

A quick glance at your financial situation.

  • How much money did I spend?
  • How much money is left?
  • Did I forget to categorize anything?
  • Quickly open a frequently used account or see a balance
  • Quick check on monthly budgets
  • What is my net worth?
  • How much liquid assets do I have?

Accounts

Chart of accounts. Create root accounts, swipe left to hide accounts, or right to create subaccount. Tap any account to see balance and transactions.

Activity

Your summary per period - weekly, monthly, quarterly or yearly.

Funding - money in. Income and refunds.

Burning - money out. Mostly expenses.

Calendar dates do not fit your needs? Enable flexible periods and set what works best for you.

Want to see set budgets? Enable budgeting for the period you'd like in settings.

GnuCash Sync

HandsOnMoney tracks transactions and account changes. When you're ready - you can synchronize HandsOnMoney with your GnuCash file.

Things that synchronize:

  • Accounts
  • Transactions
  • Commodities
  • Prices
File syncing that works:
  • iCloud works best
  • Syncthing is the best privacy choice
  • Google Drive
  • DropBox

Things that do not work:

  • Trading Accounts [will be fixed]
  • Stock Splits [will be fixed]
  • Budgets
File syncing that works:
  • OneDrive
